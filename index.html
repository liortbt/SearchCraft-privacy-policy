SearchCraft Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 29, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to SearchCraft. We are committed to protecting your privacy and transparency in how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Search queries
  • Browser and device information
  • Usage statistics
  • User preferences and settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve search functionality
  • Provide personalized search experiences
  • Enhance extension performance
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Search providers necessary for functionality
  • Legal requirements as mandated by law
  • Protection against potential security threats

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data transmissions
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

6. User Controls

You have the right to:

  • Disable or uninstall the extension
  • Reset or clear your preferences
  • Opt-out of certain data collection features

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal local storage and browser extensions APIs for functionality. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes via the extension or our website.
